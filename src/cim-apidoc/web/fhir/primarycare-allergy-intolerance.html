Resource PrimaryCare-AllergyIntolerance

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://endeavourhealth.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/primarycare-allergy-intolerance

Maturity level: Initial development.

Description

Primary Care allergy intolerance.

Risk of harmful or undesirable, physiological response which is unique to an individual and associated with exposure to a substance.

This profile is derived from AllergyIntolerance.
