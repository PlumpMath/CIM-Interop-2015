FHIR profiles

Resource PrimaryCare-Immunization

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://endeavourhealth.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/primarycare-immunization

Maturity level: Draft (for review).

Description

Primary Care immunization.

Describes the event of a patient being administered a vaccination or a record of a vaccination as reported by a patient, a clinician or another party and may include vaccine reaction information and what vaccination protocol was followed.

Definition

This profile is derived from Immunization.
