Overview

This guide is a set of publicly available resources to facilitate interoperability between all health and social care systems in the UK.

What is interoperability?

Within health and social care, interoperability means the ability of information systems to work together within and across organisational boundaries in order to advance the effective delivery of health and social care for individuals and communities.[1]
